Student Success

Leverage machine learning to predict your high school success.

We've created a questionnaire based on a machine learning model to predict your future high shcool success. Our model is based on the dataset from the Educational Longitudinal Study of 2002 (ELS: 2002). The dataset contains thousands of high school students and the parameters that shaped their high school, college, and professional careers.
